Pain points for companies

For companies often times the process of bringing a software product to life is:

  • Full of mistakes the client didn't even realize they are making
  • Too technical to fully grasp
  • Not dependable/trustworthy
  • Sometimes visible but rarely transparent
  • Ends with a subpar quality product: dated technologies used, dated look, not functional
  • Ends without a product
  • Tied to providers for maintenance and service for the rest of the product's life

We provide our clients:

  • A process where they are guided and provided with training about all the steps, to ensure they can make well-informed decisions that lead to a successful product.
  • State of the art quality solutions using the latest viable technology: Your app will be everything you ask and look good.
  • Complete visibility: We involve clients in the weekly sprint meetings to discuss current and next iterations, give them access to view workflow, provide them with an on-site developer for part of the week.
  • Complete transparency: Clients have access to software version control tools, they have full information on services and technologies used, and most importantly we provide them guidance and/or trainings on how to use them.
  • Full access to IP from start to finish.
  • Clean hand-off of product to company for maintenance: We provide on-boarding for their existing personnel, as well as assist in recruiting.
